# AI_scam_voice_detection

## Problem Statement:


### Problem — AI Voice Impersonation
Now a days scammers are impersonating as family members, relatives or friends and also using their voice with the help of AI. 
Their aim is to obtain money from people by telling emotional stories. Because the voice sounds very similar to someone we know, we believe that they genuinely need help. As a result, many people trust them and transfer money without verifying the situation. 

### Problem — Fake Authority Impersonation
Scammers also impersonate as police officers, bank officials or some government officials and ask for a settlement amount. They do not always use AI, but they create a scenario and try to convince people to send money. Because the situation sounds serious or urgent, many people get scared and transfer the money.

### Problem 3 - Threat or Extortion 
During a threat or extortion call, users often panic and do not know what to do in that situation. They get confused about whom to connect with and what steps they should take.

## One Stop Solution:
My ASVD Plugin is a powerful tool which can help to solve all of these problems. It allows the user to check whether the person they are talking to is real or fake. The system analyzes the voice and conversation to determine if the caller is genuine or not by using Machine Learning as well as Natural Language Processing.

## Working:
It works in Three steps:

### Step 1 — Voice Analysis (Machine Learning)
It uses the concept of Machine Learning. I have trained the model using various voice samples including both AI generated voices and real human voices. The model learns different audio features such as pitch, wavelength of the sound, spectrogram and different patterns in the audio signal. Using these features, the model learns to differentiate between AI generated voice and real voice.

### Step 2 — Conversation Analysis (NLP)
It uses the concept of Natural Language Processing. It marks certain words or phrases in the conversation which can warn the user that the call might be a potential scam. NLP also helps to identify extortion or threatening calls using a set of common words or patterns.

## Scam Scenarios Covered List:
